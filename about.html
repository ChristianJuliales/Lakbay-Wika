About Lakbay-Wika

Lakbay-Wika is an intuitive and accessible mobile and web application designed to seamlessly translate English phrases into Tagalog and the eight major Filipino dialects. By offering accurate, text-based translations, it empowers users to communicate effortlessly with locals, bridging language gaps and enhancing cultural connections. Whether you're a traveler or simply looking to explore the rich diversity of the Philippines, Lakbay-Wika makes language barriers a thing of the past.
